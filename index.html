Demo of many features from the OU¹ app. Not concerned with the nickels and dimes of accounting ledgers, OU¹ keeps track of who owes who in the social currency of drinks, lunches, and dinners using electronic IOUs. I was the co-creator of OU¹ app and the co-owner of Big Machine in the Sky, LLC (the parent company.)
